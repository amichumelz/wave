FAQ

Quick answers about booking tours, car rental, and how we work with you from enquiry to arrival.

How do I book a tour or a car?

Tap View package on any tour card or vehicle — a popup opens with full details and a short booking form. Pick your package where options are available, review the summary, then send it through WhatsApp for our team to confirm price, slot, and payment — nothing is charged on the website.

Do I pay online on this website?

No. The forms are for enquiries only. Rates depend on season, fleet, and operator schedules. We send a clear quote; you decide before any deposit or payment arrangement off-site.

Where can you pick up or deliver a rental car?

Most guests use LGK airport, Kuah, Pantai Cenang, or their hotel — tell us in the form and we’ll quote accordingly. Island-wide options depend on the partner for that vehicle class.

What if the weather cancels a boat trip?

Marine activities follow skipper briefings. If a trip is unsafe, operators reschedule or refund per their policy — we communicate options as soon as we know.

Can you customise a multi-day itinerary?

Yes — tell us your dates and interests on WhatsApp. We can combine stays, day trips, mangrove tours, and transport into one coherent plan.
