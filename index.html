Welcome to Langkawi

Malaysia’s island of legends — jungle, beaches, and mangroves in one easy loop.

Langkawi is a UNESCO Global Geopark: limestone peaks, telaga waterfalls, and fishing villages between stretches of Andaman blue. Whether it’s your first visit or your fifth, the island is best explored slowly — with room to stop for coconut ice cream or a sudden sunset.

Wave Langkawi is your local host for island travel: routes, tips, and when you’re ready — a straightforward car rental desk at LGK and Pantai Cenang so you can drive on your own schedule.

What your rate includes

  • Unlimited mileage on standard rentals (cars listed on this site)
  • Basic coverage as required by law; upgrades available at pickup
  • 24/7 phone support and replacement vehicle in case of breakdown*
  • Free map & recommended island routes from our team

*Within Langkawi island. Full wording in rental agreement.

Typical fees (transparent)

  • Security deposit: RM 300–800 (pre-authorisation on card), released after return.
  • Late return: Charged per hour or full day at published daily rate.
  • Fuel: Return with same level as pickup; refuelling service available.
  • Cross-border: Vehicles may not leave Langkawi — ferries excluded.

Driver requirements & documents

You must have:

  • Valid driving licence held for at least 1 year (English or certified translation)
  • Passport or Malaysian NRIC for identity check
  • Credit or debit card for security deposit (some categories)
  • Minimum age 23 for economy / MPV; 25 for luxury (exceptions by approval)

Airport pickup (LGK):

Our representative meets you in arrivals with a sign — include flight number and ETA when you book so we can track delays. After hours pickups may incur a small fee (shown before confirmation).

    These terms summarise a typical Langkawi car rental agreement. They are for demonstration on this website — replace with counsel-approved wording for production.

    Rental agreement

    The renter must comply with Malaysian traffic law and JPJ rules. The vehicle remains property of Wave Langkawi or its partners until full payment and checkout.

    Deposit

    A security deposit is pre-authorised on card or held in cash; it is released after return subject to damage inspection and toll/fuel adjustments.

    Cancellation

    Cancellations 48+ hours before pickup: deposit refunded in full. Within 48 hours: partial or full retention as stated at booking. No-show may forfeit full rental.

    Insurance

    Basic coverage is included as required. Optional CDW reduces excess; exclusions include tyre/rim damage, interior stains, lost keys, and driving under influence.

    Use of vehicle

    Vehicles are for roads on Langkawi island only; ferrying the car off-island is prohibited unless written approval is given.
